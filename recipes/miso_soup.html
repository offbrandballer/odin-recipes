Miso Soup Recipe

recipe found here

Ingredients dashi stock miso paste tofu(optional) seaweed(optional) green onion(optional) Directions put about half of the water in the pot and bring the water to a boil. put the dashi stock in the pot. if you use tofu or seaweed, cut the ingredients into bite-sized pieces, place the ingredients in the pot and bring to a boil. Melt the miso in the water and bring the water to a boil. serve into small bowls. if you use green onion, slice them and put in a small bowls.
